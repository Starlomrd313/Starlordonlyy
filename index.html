Sumerah,

Tujhe kya pata ke subah uthte hi tera khayal aata hai. Na main bhoolne ki koshish karta hoon, na yaad karne ki — tu toh bas wahan hoti hai, beghair bulaye.

Maine kabhi kaha nahi. Shayad kabhi keh bhi na sakoon. Par yeh alfaaz sirf itna kehna chahte hain — teri khushi meri dua hai. Teri muskaan meri neend se zyaada qeemti hai.

Jo tujhe khushi de, woh ho jaye. Bas.

Ek Tarfa Mohabbat

Ek tarfa pyar sabse saaf hota hai.
Ismein koi shart nahi, koi umeed nahi.

Jo sachcha aashiq hota hai —
woh apni mehbooba ki khushi ki dua karta hai,
chahe uss khushi mein uski jagah na ho.

Woh jo bol nahi saka, woh likh diya.
Woh jo likh nahi saka, woh aankhon mein rakh liya.

Tujhe paana agar naseeb mein nahi,
toh teri khushi hi meri manzil hai.
Tujhe dekhna, tujhe muskurate dekhna —
yahi kaafi hai mere liye,
